Newbie - Intro 2: What's your name? (A0002)

A:你好。
Nǐhǎo.
Hello.
B:你好，你叫什么名字？
Nǐhǎo, nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
Hello. What's your name?
A:我叫王平。你呢？
Wǒ jiào Wáng Píng. Nǐ ne?
My name is Wang Ping. How about you?
B:我叫李丽。
Wǒ jiào Lǐ Lì.
My name is Li Li.
